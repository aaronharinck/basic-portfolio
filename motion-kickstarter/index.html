What is kickstarter

Kickstarter helps artists, musicians, filmmakers, designers, and other creators find the resources and support they need to make their ideas a reality. To date, tens of thousands of creative projects — big and small — have come to life with the support of the Kickstarter community.
Assignment

The assignment was to create a Kickstarter motion video for a Kickstarter project without one. I was required to create a visual representation to give potential backers a clear image as to what the Kickstarter project was all about.

Approach

I chose the Kickstarter project called innovaTEEN: a platform that connects high school students with innovative product ideas with the right business mentors who may be interested.

I picked an abstract visual style (as a reference to all the abstract but inovative ideas teenagers may have) while also making sure the abstract forms could still be easily intterpreted and bring over a clear message.
